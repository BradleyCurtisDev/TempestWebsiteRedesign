Who We Are

A Leading UK Debt Recovery Agent

Tempest Financial Services Ltd. was founded in 2011 with a clear purpose: to give UK businesses a smarter, fairer way to recover outstanding debt. Our model is simple — we work on your behalf and you keep every penny you're owed.

We understand that every client's situation is unique. That's why we offer bespoke recovery solutions built around your business, your terms, and your relationships. From invoice acceleration to legal action support, we handle the complexity so you don't have to.

Our Services

A full suite of debt recovery and payment management solutions, tailored to your business needs.

Debt Recovery at No Cost

We recover your outstanding debt at zero cost to you. Our fee is collected from the debtor, meaning you receive the full balance owed — nothing less.

Bespoke Recovery Solutions

Every business is different. We design tailored recovery strategies that align with your client relationships, industry, and commercial objectives.

Arbitration Assistance

When disputes arise, we guide you through the arbitration process with expert support — helping you reach a fair resolution without costly court proceedings.

Legal Action Support

Should legal proceedings become necessary, we provide guidance and support throughout — ensuring you understand your options and are fully prepared at every stage.

Free T&C Review

Invoice Payment Acceleration

Speed up your cash flow with our invoice acceleration service — we work proactively to get your outstanding invoices paid faster, keeping your business moving.
