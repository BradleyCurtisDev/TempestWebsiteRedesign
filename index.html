Why enforcement costs are recoverable

Under the Late Payment of Commercial Debts (Interest) Act 1998 and through the civil courts, Tempest Financial is legally entitled to claim all reasonable recovery and enforcement costs directly from the debtor. This means court fees, bailiff charges, solicitor costs, and our own recovery fees are added to the debt and pursued against the debtor — not deducted from your settlement.

As a debtor, ignoring a County Court Judgement or enforcement action does not make the debt go away. Costs compound with interest, enforcement options escalate, and credit ratings are affected. The most cost-effective course of action for a debtor is always to engage early and resolve the matter before further action is taken.
